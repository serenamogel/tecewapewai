How Much Is The Tattoo Artist Worth?

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Thursday, April 25, 2024

Between her reality TV shows and cosmetics line, Kat Von D has amassed quite a fortune in both her professional and personal life. After a failed marriage to controversial Ink Master alum Oliver Peck, Von D wed Prayers frontman Rafael Reyes in 2018 and welcomed a son that same year.

Von D seems to have found happiness, and the birth of her son prompted her to reevaluate her business ventures. "This past year has been one of great change for me," she stated in a January 2020 Instagram post, explaining her decision to sell her cosmetics company. "As many of you know, I gave birth to my beautiful baby boy, launched my vegan shoe line, and am now busy prepping to release my long-awaited album... It has become clear to me that I just can't do everything at the maximum capacity." 

She announced her vegan shoe brand in 2019; according to InStyle, some of the shoes are made in Italy out of apples. The line also benefits no-kill animal shelters.

As for her music career, Von D seems to be following in husband Reyes' footsteps, teasing an upcoming album and an international tour in her Instagram post. Von D certainly has the funds to explore a new career as a singer: the star is reportedly worth $20 million, according to Celebrity Net Worth.
