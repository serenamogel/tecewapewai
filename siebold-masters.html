Siebold (Japanese: ズミ Zumi) is a Pokémon Trainer in Pokémon Masters EX, based on the character of the same name in the core series games. He forms a sync pair with Clawitzer. The sync pair can be obtained through the sync pair scout. This pair could be raised to 6★ EX beginning on February 25, 2022.

Siebold is also available in his Holiday attire, partnered with his Octillery. This pair can be obtained through a limited sync pair scout. This pair could be raised to 6★ EX beginning on March 1, 2022.

Trivia

  • Siebold (Holiday 2019) has been available in more banners than any other seasonal sync pair, having appeared in 10 different banners.
  • Siebold (Holiday 2019) & Octillery are the only seasonal sync pair to take over a year to gain the ability to reach 6★ EX, with a total of 814 days between their release and the date they had 6★ EX unlocked.
  • All versions of Siebold use special voice-over lines upon entering a team that contains certain Trainers (regardless of their partner Pokémon). Those Trainers include Grant, Valerie, Wallace, and Mallow.
    • Siebold also uses special voice-over lines upon winning a battle with Mallow or Wallace in the same team.

Origin

Siebold's design is based on his appearance in Pokémon X and Y. Clawitzer is one of the Pokémon that Siebold uses in the core series games. Clawitzer's Dex entry comes from Pokémon Ultra Sun.

Siebold is not seen to own an Octillery outside this game. Octillery's Dex entry comes from Pokémon Ultra Sun.
