Hines Ward Biography

Hines Ward is a Korean-American football coach and former football player who was born in Seoul, South Korea, and raised in Atlanta and East Point, Georgia. Currently, he works at CNN Sports as a studio analyst, where he provide analysis and commentary on major sporting events and breaking news across all CNN platforms including CNN Digital, CNN International, CNNMoney, and CNN/U.S.

    Hines Ward Career

    Ward currently works at CNN Sports as a studio analyst, where he provide analysis and commentary on major sporting events and breaking news across all CNN platforms including CNN Digital, CNN International, CNNMoney, and CNN/U.S.

    He also works at HLN as a sports contributor for HLN’s program ‘Morning Express’ with Robin Meade. He is also known as a two-time Super Bowl champion. He served as an analyst for NBC’s Sunday primetime NFL show, Football Night in America from 2012-2015.

    Previously, Hines worked as a wide receiver of the National Football League (NFL), and he played college football at the University of Georgia and was drafted by the Pittsburgh Steelers in the third round of the 1998 NFL Draft.
