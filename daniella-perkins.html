Meet-Daniella Perkins, a social media star, and an actress. Know about her relationship status.

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Monday, August 12, 2024

Daniella Perkins is an American social media star and an arising actress who has starred in the Television series such as Middle Squad Moguls, Knight Squad, Legendary Dudas, Blurt, etc. Daniella is gaining fame from the social account, Perkins Sisters, which she created with her sibling Devenity Perkins.

The beautiful and talented Daniella was born on June 13, 2000, in Anaheim, Califonia, USA, under the zodiac sign Gemini. Daniella's father passed away back on January 17, 2020. 

Daniella Perkins Current Relationship Status

The 20-years-old social media personality Daniella currently lives a single life without having any romantic affairs. At the moment, she is busy making her career. So, this might be the reason behind her singleness.

Or the lady might be searching the right guy to start her romantic life. Daniella is very friendly to her friends, co-stars, and family members with whom she has also shared pictures on her official Instagram account.

Daniella shares a good bond as a true friend with her Knight Squad co-star Owen Joyner. Back on June 8, 2019, Daniella and Owen attended the Nickelodeon's Second Annual SlimeFest together. Joyner and Perkins are also featured on the cover of Pop Star!

Dated Zach Clayton

The Middle Squad Moguls cast Daniella previously dated Zach Clayton. Like Daniella, Zach is also a social media star and a musician who has released the songs like Kick It With Me, Odd Petals, How Bout Dat, Off White, and several others. 

Clayton and Perkins dated temporarily back in 2017. After dating for several months, the former pair revealed the news of their separation via a Twitter post. On September 10, 2017, Daniell tweeted, "Zach & I decided it would be best for us to just be friends. We both have so much going on and it's best for both of us."

Zach also announced their split about by tweeting. But he blamed a fellow actor Jace Norman for the reason behind their separation. Clayton tweeted, "how does it feel coming in between people's relationships @Jacenorman."

However, Daniella denied Zach's allegations. She said that the breakup had nothing to do with Jace. For the information, the actor Jace and an actress Daniella are also all set to perform in the Nickelodeon's upcoming series Inside Voice.

Furthermore, there is also a rumor that an actress Daniella once linked with the American singer, songwriter, Marteen Estevez. However, neither Daniella nor Marteen have openly clarified about their relationship. 

Net Worth

Daniella has an estimated net worth of around $800 thousand which she accumulated from her appearance in several movies and TV shows.

In the 2018 actions/comedy Television movie Blurt, Daniella has played a role as Milly Turkel. Similarly, Perkins has starred in the films Senior Slashed and Girls Meet World. 

Besides acting, Daniella has also made a paid partnership with the make-up company Maybelline, hair & styling products Aussie, and with beverages company CocaCola as well.

Social Media Appearance

The primary reason behind the 5-feet-1-inch (155 cm) tall Daniella's prominence was because of using social media platforms positively in an inspiring way. Perkins has 990 thousand and 42 thousand followers on her Instagram and Twitter account respectively.

Similarly, Daniella and her sister Devenity's joint TikTok id (Perkins Sisters) has amassed 2.6 million followers on her TikTok account. Likewise, their collaborative You-Tube channel has 89.1 thousand subscribers. 

Not only that, but Daniella also runs her official You-Tube channel (Daniella Perkins). There she has received 853,000 subscribers. As per the social blade, the channel earns $219 - $3.5K per month and makes between $2.6K - $42K per year.
