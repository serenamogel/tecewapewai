The world of sports was shaken by a devastating incident on August 20, 2023, as Jori Jones, a bright young NCAA hockey player, lost her life in a car accident in Minnesota. 

Her obituary hasn’t been shared since she passed away two days ago, and her loved ones are awaiting details about her funeral arrangements.

This devastating incident not only claimed Jori’s life but also left her teammates – Gianna Gasparini, Kayla Bluhm, and Lily Mortenson – in shock.

The circumstances surrounding the accident, the heartfelt tributes, and the indomitable spirit of Jori herself have left a profound impact on those who knew her.

Who was Jori Jones?

Jori Jones was not just an athlete but a radiant soul who radiated positivity and touched countless lives. 

 She was a rising star in college hockey, but her love for the game started long before that.

Jori was part of the Roseville Area Youth Hockey Association when she was younger. 

This was special because her dad was a coach there too. They shared a special connection through their love for hockey.

But Jori wasn’t just about playing hockey. She had a smile that could light up a room and a kind heart that made people feel good. 

Her happiness was infectious, and wherever she went, people felt happier around her.

 Jori genuinely cared about her community and sought to make a positive difference in the lives of those around her.

Her actions and warm demeanour left a profound mark on everyone fortunate enough to cross her path. 

Through her example, she demonstrated that being a good person held equal importance to excelling in sports.

Jori Jones’car accident update

The Gustavus Adolphus College women’s hockey team was hit hard by accident, as Jori was an integral part of their close-knit unit. 

The collision occurred as the team members travelled on Highway 40 in a Chevrolet Equinox, colliding with a southbound Dodge Caravan. 

Jori’s loss was profound, leaving her teammates grieving during a team bonding trip in North Dakota.

Lily Mortenson, one of Jori’s teammates, struggled to find words to describe the impact Jori had on their lives. 

The kindest of persons with an illuminating smile, Jori’s absence left an emptiness that was difficult to process.

Lily’s poignant words underscored the depth of the connections formed within the Gustavus Women’s Hockey team.

Jori Jones car accident photos

After the collision took place, the moments that immediately followed were captured by photographs. 

The pictures capture the damaged vehicles, the surrounding environment, and the aftermath of the collision, revealing the extent of the tragedy.

Their visual narrative powerfully conveys the gravity of the situation, allowing viewers to grasp the sequence of events and the emotions tied to them. 

GoFundMe Page for Jori Jones’ funeral expenses

After the Jori Jones sudden passing, , the community rallied together to provide support in a tangible way. 

Roseville Area Youth Hockey Association president Gretchen Hopeman initiated a GoFundMe page to alleviate the financial burden of Jori’s funeral expenses. 

Described as a “fantastic person and student” by Andrea Schmidt, activities director for Roseville Area High School, Jori’s impact was felt far and wide.

The GoFundMe page’s mission was clear: to honour Jori’s memory and extend a helping hand to her grieving family. 

The heartfelt post on the page spoke of Jori’s infectious smile, genuine spirit, and her significant role in the community. 

The call for contributions resonated, and the page gained traction rapidly. 

With a goal of $85,000, the page had already amassed $72,103 from 852 generous donations, underscoring the deep connections Jori forged during her lifetime.

Jori’s legacy and tributes 

Sports venue Hidden Pines Ranch took to social media to pay tribute to the late athlete, expressing gratitude for having had the chance to work with her. 

Jori’s dream of being a camp counsellor was realized at the ranch, and her impact was profound. 

The post from Hidden Pines Ranch exemplified the sentiment echoed by all who encountered Jori – to know her was to love her.

Jori’s involvement in sports went beyond the rink. A member of the women’s hockey team at Gustavus Adolphus College. 

She was also a representative of Roseville in various games and events, proudly upholding the values of the National Honor Society. 

Her father’s role as a coach in the Roseville Area Youth Hockey Association added another layer of meaning to her athletic journey.

Honoring Jori Jones

Jori’s memory continues to shine brightly in the hearts of those who knew her. 

The team’s call to place hockey sticks on front porches serves as a poignant reminder of Jori’s love for the sport and her enduring presence. 

An online fundraiser for her family further underscores the unity of the community in times of sorrow.

In the wake of the accident, questions about who Jori Jones was only scratch the surface of her impactful journey.
