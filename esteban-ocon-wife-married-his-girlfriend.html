Apart from his striking racing career, fans are interested to learn about Esteban Ocon Wife and his dating history. Esteban is a notable racer in the French racing community. 

Esteban Ocon is a French racing driver who competes in Formula 1 for team Alpine. He made his Formula 1 debut in 2016 and has since become a prominent figure in the sport.

After winning the 2015 GP3 Series and 2014 FIA Formula 3 European Champion, Ocon experienced a successful junior career.

The racer replaced Rio Haryanto in the Belgian Grand Prix 2016 and for Manor Racing, he made his Formula One debut after winning the GP3 title.

Likewise, to line up with Sergio Pérez in the 2018 and 2017 Formula 1 season, he transferred to Force India/Racing Point in 2017.

After Racing Point let Ocon go at the conclusion of the 2018 season, he got his role as a reserve driver for Mercedes in 2019 before switching to Renault in 2020.

Furthermore, at the Hungarian Grand Prix 2021, he earned his first Formula One win.

Despite failing to win a race or finish on the podium in 2022, he managed to finish eighth in the drivers’ standings with 92 points, 11 points ahead of teammate Fernando Alonso, aiding Alpine to finish 4th in the Constructors’ Championship.
