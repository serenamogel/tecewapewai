DescriptionKoga's daughter and a ninja Gym Leader, she uses Poison-type Pokémon out of respect for her father. She also likes to argue like him from time to time.

Janine (Japanese: アンズ Anzu) is a Pokémon Trainer in Pokémon Masters EX, based on the character of the same name in the core series games. She forms a sync pair with Ariados. This sync pair was first made available since the game release.

Janine also forms a sync pair with her Crobat. This pair can be obtained by exchanging a BP Sync Pair Voucher and was first made available on August 28, 2022. This pair could be raised to 6★ EX beginning on March 29, 2023, though this pair lacks an EX style.

Trivia

  • Despite having the Kanto theme skill, Janine is listed among Johto characters on the sync pair story list.
  • Janine's Crobat shares its attacking moves with her father's Crobat.

Origin

Janine's design is based on her appearance in Pokémon HeartGold and SoulSilver. Janine uses Ariados during her Gym battle with the player in the Johto-based core series games. Ariados's Dex entry comes from Pokémon Ultra Sun.

Crobat is Janine's signature Pokémon in the core series games. Crobat's Dex entry comes from Pokémon Ultra Sun.
