Anna Yourkin is a Canadian author known for being the mother of Luka Magnotta, a former porn artist and convicted murderer. Her son gained international notoriety in 2012 after he was accused of stabbing Jun Lin, a Chinese student.

Early Life and Family

  • Anna Yourkin was born in Canada in 1967, making her around 52 years old.
  • Information about her parents is not available, and she completed her education at a local school.

Personal Life and Relationships

  • Anna Yourkin met Donald Newman, her love interest, when she was 16 years old.
  • They had three children together – Luka Magnotta, Conrad, and Melissa.
  • Anna was known for her obsession with cleanliness and had some peculiar behaviors, like not allowing her children to use public washrooms.
  • She separated from Donald after he was diagnosed with schizophrenia in 1994.

Career and Writing

  • Anna Yourkin wrote a book titled “My Son the Killer: The Untold Story of Luka Magnotta & I Lunatic 1 Ice Pick,” where she described her relationship with her son.
  • The book, co-authored by Brian Whitney, delves into the events surrounding her son’s crimes.

Appearance and Documentary

  • Anna appeared in the Netflix documentary series “Don’t F*ck With Cats: Hunting an Internet Killer,” which explored the online manhunt for Luka Magnotta.

Luka Magnotta’s Crimes

  • Luka Magnotta was involved in porn films and occasionally worked as a male escort and stripper.

Conclusion

Anna Yourkin’s life took a dark turn when her son Luka Magnotta became infamous for his heinous crimes. Despite the shocking events surrounding her family, she has shared her side of the story in her book and interviews, shedding some light on the difficult journey she has faced as a mother.
