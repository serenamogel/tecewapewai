Scarlett Johansson Ex-Husband: Meet Ryan Reynolds

Johansson was first married to Canadian actor Ryan Reynolds in September 2008. The couple had been dating since 2007 and tied the knot in a private ceremony in Canada.

However, their marriage was short-lived, and they announced their separation in December 2010. The divorce was finalized in July 2011.

Following her split from Reynolds, Johansson dated several men, including Sean Penn, Nate Naylor, and Romain Dauriac.

She was first spotted with Penn in January 2011, and the couple was seen together at several events, including Reese Witherspoon’s wedding.

However, their relationship was short-lived, and they split between June of the same year.

Johansson then dated ad executive Nate Naylor in 2012 but broke up after a year of dating.

She then began dating French journalist Romain Dauriac in November 2012, and the couple got engaged in September 2013.

They welcomed their daughter, Rose Dorothy, in September 2014 and got married in a private ceremony in October of the same year.

However, Johansson’s second marriage also didn’t last, and she and Dauriac announced their separation in January 2017.

They finalized their divorce in September 2017, and Johansson has since moved on and found love with Colin Jost.
