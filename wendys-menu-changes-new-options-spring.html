Wendy's menu changes from today with three new options - and fans will love 'grab and go' addition

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Wednesday, July 17, 2024

WENDY’S is updating its menu for spring with three new items that correlate with the season.

The fast-food restaurant hopes to grab the attention of customers with its new "grab and go" option, along with some refreshing choices that will join the menu starting March 28.

The three new items on the menu include a Blueberry Pomegranate Lemonade, Grilled Chicken Ranch Wrap, and Grilled Chicken Cobb Salad.

"We are always listening to our fans and introducing exciting new menu items to give them exactly what they are craving," Wendy's Global Chief Marketing Officer Carl Loredo said in a news release.

Here's a breakdown of the three spring-inspired items now available to fans.

1. BLUEBERRY POMEGRANATE LEMONADE

The lemonade will cost $2.29 and is said to have tart notes of pomegranate along with a sweet touch of blueberry in Dave’s Craft Lemonade.

"Our Dave's Craft Lemonades have been a fan-favorite menu item for years, and our all-natural lemonade foundation allows us to bring flavor innovation like no one else in the business," Loredo said of the new flavor.

2. GRILLED CHICKEN RANCH WRAP

This new item has been described as the “perfect grab-and-go meal” and will reportedly cost about $6.29.

The chicken wrap comes with grilled pieces of chicken, shredded cheddar cheese, romaine lettuce, and ranch sauce.

"At Wendy's, we know ranch," Vice President of Culinary Innovation John Li said.

"There are many who've tried to master the grilled chicken wrap, but we're not snacking around with our offering."

3. GRILLED CHICKEN COBB SALAD

Wendy’s has been known to offer different seasonal salads that have come with mandarin oranges or even apples in the past.

This time, the fast-food chain is introducing a cobb salad version.

It will come with romaine lettuce, chopped applewood smoked bacon, diced tomato, grilled chicken, crispy fried onions, diced egg, and ranch dressing.

The salad will cost $6.29 like the chain's other salads.

"The freshest made Cobb in the game,” Wendy’s dubbed the item.

OTHER MENU UPDATES

The fast-food chain also brought a fish sandwich into the mix for lent called the Crispy Panko Fish.

Wendy’s recently brought back its vanilla-flavored frosty which replaced the strawberry frosty flavor that was introduced for the first time last year.

It also is set to sell its beloved chili in grocery stores.

Wendy's also has plans to start implementing a delivery pick-up window and new-generation technology in its restaurants.
