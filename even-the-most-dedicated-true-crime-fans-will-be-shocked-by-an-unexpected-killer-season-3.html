Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Saturday, March 16, 2024

Unpredictable is an understatement.

Oxygen's true-crime series An Unexpected Killer is back for its third and most shocking season yet. Brand new episodes begin March 4, and based on this exclusive sneak peek, viewers will be second-guessing everything as they follow along with experienced homicide investigators who believe they're inches away from solving a complicated case, only to come across something entirely unanticipated.
