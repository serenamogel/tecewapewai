All the Toaster Strudel Flavors, Ranked

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Wednesday, August 14, 2024

The best Toaster Strudel flavors are not hard to find. Pretty much every grocery store is stocked with several varieties of some of the most delicious store bought pastries you can buy. That being said, some Toaster Strudel flavors are better than others. 

We tasted every Toaster Strudel flavor that is currently on the market—including Toaster Scrambles!—in an attempt to find the most elite freezer section pastry. Breakfast lovers, this is a ranking for you!

Toaster Strudel!

Blueberry

This Toaster Strudel flavor gets a 0/10 because we couldn’t find it anywhere. It’s sad because this was my favorite flavor growing up! I searched and searched and no stores in our area had it. I even found Reddit threads of people complaining about how this flavor is impossible to get. Please fix this, Pillsbury!

Credit: Merc / Toaster Strudel

Rating:

0/10

There’s no point in buying this. It doesn’t taste nearly as good as a cinnamon roll and it is way worse than all the other Toaster Strudel flavors. I know it sounds like it would be good, but leave these on the shelf.

Toaster Strudel!

AppleInstacart

Apple is a step up from Cinnamon Roll for sure, but it’s nothing to write home about. If you absolutely love apple pie and want it for breakfast, try these! If not, you can skip this Toaster Strudel flavor.

Credit: Merc / Instacart

6/10

Toaster Strudel!

StrawberryTarget

Strawberry is a classic Toaster Strudel flavor and a real crowd pleaser. It’s perfectly sweet and fruity. Both kids and adults can get behind this. It’s a tad basic, but what’s so wrong with that?

Credit: Merc / Target

Rating:

8.5/10

Sporks

Toaster Strudel!

Cream Cheese & StrawberryInstacart

For my money, I’d skip the regular strawberry and get the cream cheese and strawberry Toaster Strudel flavor instead. The addition of the cream cheese makes it feel like a fancy danish from a bakery. The creamy cream cheese and the fruity strawberry go great with the flaky pastry and gooey icing.

Credit: Merc / Instacart

Rating:

9/10

Sporks

Toaster Strudel!

Sausage Toaster ScramblesTarget

The savory breakfast Toaster Strudel flavors (aka Toaster Scrambles) are a revelation. They are so incredibly delicious. If you told me these were pastries from an expensive bakery that you had to wait in line for a long time to buy, I’d believe you! The sausage is savory, the cheese is rich, the eggs are fluffy, and the pastry is melty. I have literally no notes.

Credit: Merc / Target

Rating:

10/10

Sporks

Toaster Strudel!

Bacon Toaster ScramblesInstacart

The bacon Toaster Strudel is my personal favorite. The smoky bacon and rich, cheesy eggs make the ideal filling. As always, the pastry is light and airy. This is the kind of breakfast you dream about waking up to in the morning.

Credit: Merc / Instacart

Rating:

10/10

Sporks

Toaster Strudel!

RaspberryInstacart

Raspberry is, hands down, the best sweet Toaster Strudel flavor. The filling has a slight tartness that the other fruit flavors lack, which adds so much to the pastry. It’s a little more complex. This is not only the best Toaster Strudel flavor, it’s one of the best pastries you can buy at the grocery store. I mean that!

Credit: Merc / Instacart

Rating:

10/10
