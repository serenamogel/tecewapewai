Who Is Fujiko Rose? See The Artist On Wikipedia

Fujiko Rose is pronounced as one of the most highly anticipated and exciting artist emerging in the 21st century.

She is from London, England.

Rose is the winner of SkyArts Landscape Artist of the Year Winner in 2019.

Fujiko’s Japanese heritage had a lot of impact on her art.

Everything About Fujiko Rose’s Family

Fujiko Rose’s family are from Japan. They probably migrated to the United Kingdom.

Fujiko was born here in London in 1997.

Due to her Japanese heritage. it had massive influence on my mind and later it ignited her artistic abilities. She was fascinated by reading manga as a child.

Her mother is also interested in art and design.

Furthermore, she has been silent about the identity of her family members. This is because she wants to protect their privacy. Once people’s family enters into spotlight then it becomes quite a hard task to cope up with media attention.
