Age, Height, Ethnicity, and Trivia of Charlie McDermott

Charles Joseph McDermott Jr. was born on April 6, 1990, to parents Barbara (Reilly) and Charlie McDermott. Charlie, who is 33 years old and of Irish descent, stands 5 feet and 7 inches tall (1.7m). Grace and Erin are his two sisters.

He received his diploma from PA Leadership Charter School (PALCS). Prior to that, he briefly attended Salesianum High School in Willmington, Delaware. McDermott moved to Los Angeles at the age of 16 to pursue his dream of becoming an actor. He’s frequently mistaken for Charlie McDermott, a New Zealand actor with the same name.
