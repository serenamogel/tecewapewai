Jahi Di’Allo Winston is an American actor born on November 30, 2003, in Atlanta, Georgia, USA. He is known for his roles in various movies and TV series such as Charm City Kings (2020), Queen & Slim (2019), and The Dead Don’t Die (2019).

In Charm City Kings, Winston plays the role of Mouse, a 14-year-old boy who wants to join a group of Baltimore dirt-bike riders called the Midnight Clique. His older brother, who was the group’s top rider, died tragically. Mouse’s mother and his police mentor try to help him, but when the Midnight Clique’s leader takes him under his wing, Mouse gets drawn into a dangerous lifestyle.

Winston achieved nomination awards three times and has won recognition as a Gen Z Hollywood Style Icon. He just done playing role in the film i.e., We Have a Ghost (2023) as Kevin Presley. With some experienced actors like, Anthony Mackie & David Harbour.
