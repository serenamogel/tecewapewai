Shop Taylor Swifts Coachella Pleated Skirt 2024

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Sunday, September 22, 2024

Taylor Swift isn’t performing at Coachella this year, but that doesn’t mean she wasn’t the star of the show. Fans spotted Swift and her famous NFL boyfriend, Travis Kelce, for weekend one in the desert of Indio, California. They were snuggled up supporting the singer’s fellow musician friends, Ice Spice, who appeared on Taylor’s Karma track last year, and longtime friend/songwriter Jack Antonoff — lead singer of the popular music group Bleachers. The couple were photographed backstage, then headed to the crowd during Ice Spice’s performance.

With Coachella being one of the most anticipated fashion music festivals yearly, Swift and Kelce certainly dressed up for the event. The pop star opted for an all-black grunge look with Gucci sneakers, a Stella McCartney shoulder bag, a green New Heights show hat (Travis Kelce’s podcast promo merch), an oversized street-style jacket with two off-white stripes, and a black pleated mini skirt.

Halara’s Breezeful High Waisted Pleated Skirt offers a 2-in-1 side pocket and adjustable buckle. The skirt is currently 13 percent off for $34.95, with an option of 9 alternative colors to black — so that you can wear it even if you’re missing Coachella this year. Pleated mini skirts were extremely popular in the 90s — if you’ve ever watched Clueless, then you know exactly what we mean—and seem to be making a quick comeback.

Kelce went for more of a mismatched vibe, sporting a purple plaid long-sleeve over a white t-shirt, striped flowing pants with varying blues, a green bandana, classic Converse, a white Happy Gilmore hat, and sunglasses. The stylish couple was pictured in a sweet PDA moment from TMZ during the performances. Swift is currently gearing up for her next album release, named The Tortured Poets Department, on April 19. Now that we are thinking about it, her Coachella outfit really matches the black-and-white theme of the upcoming album drop…
