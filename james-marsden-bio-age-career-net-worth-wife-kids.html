Who is James Marsden?

James Marsden is an actor, singer, and former model from the United States. Marsden made his acting debut as a guest star on the television programs Touched by an Angel, Party of Five, Saved by the Bell: The New Class, and Bella Mafia.

James Marsden bio

The son of James Luther Marsden and Kathleen (née Scholtz) Marsden, James Marsden was born in Stillwater, Oklahoma.

He has Scottish, English, German, and French ancestry.

When he was nine years old, his mother and his father—a food safety expert for LexiGene Industries—divorced.

James Marsden siblings

Two younger sisters and two brothers make up his four siblings. His brothers’ names are Robbie Marsden and Jeff Marsden. His sisters’ names are Elizabeth Marsden and Jennifer Marsden.

James Marsden education

He attended Oklahoma City’s Hefner Middle School and Putnam City North High School before continuing his education at Oklahoma State University to pursue a degree in broadcast journalism.

Additionally, he belonged to the fraternity Delta Tau Delta. After one and a half years, Marsden departed, choosing to relocate to Los Angeles in order to further his acting career.
