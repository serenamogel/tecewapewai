Orson Salazar: Who is Paz Vega's husband?

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Saturday, July 27, 2024

Orson Salazar is the husband of Paz Vega. He is a Venezuelan businessman and serial entrepreneur. Paz Vega whose real name is María de la Paz Campos Trigos is a Spanish actress. She gained much traction for her performance in the comedy television series 7 vidas. Her film credits include Sex and Lucia, Spanglish, 10 Items or Less, All Roads Lead to Rome, Acts of Vengeance, and Rambo: Last Blood.

Orson Salazar: Profile Summary

Orson Salazar gained much popularity after his marriage to the famous actress. According to his IMDB profile, he is known for MasterChef Celebrity and Premios Goya 33 edición. He was credited as a Guest and an Audience in MasterChef Celebrity and Premios Goya 33 edición respectively.

Paz Vega and her Husband Orson Salzar have been married for 20 years

 Paz Vega and her husband Orson Salazar got married in 2002 at a wedding held in Caracas, Orson’s hometown. They married in a beautiful ceremony in the presence of their friends, families, and loved ones. They have been married for about decades now with no rumors of divorce or separation. The two first met on a Saturday night at the Soho Boutique Castillo de Santa Catalina in the Andalusian city and fell in love.

They now have three children.  Their children are Ava Salazar, Lenon Salazar, and Orson Salazar Jr. They had their first child, a son, in May 2007. Their second child, a daughter, was born in July 2009. Their third child, another son, was born in August 2010.

On the Anniversary of their two decades together, Orson organized a party through his event company Bulubú, which teamed up with the Malaga Film Festival to organize an evening full of glamor as reported by HOLA.

“Sometimes I think that I love you more than what I have been able to show you,” the Venezuelan businessman told his wife Paz Vega with great romanticism. He went on -“But hey, suddenly they have been together for 20 years and I intend to continue doing everything I can“, added Paz Vega’s husband Orson Salazar to some words full of tenderness, which quickly and how could it be otherwise, has had a response from his wife. “Loving you… Let’s go for another twenty walking together… never let me go”

The couple gave also been spotted at various events together. They were pictured together on the carpet with their children when they attend Cirque Du Soleil ‘Kooza’ premiere on October 29, 2019, in Madrid, Spain. They have also been spotted at the beach having a couple’s moment together alone.

Salazar also attended the Italian Night ceremony during Marrakesh 6th International Film festival in Marrakesh, Morocco on December 02, 2006 with her wife. Salazar once again was at his wife’s side when she attended the Taormina Film Festival, in Italy, to receive an award that recognizes her for her talent as an interpreter. She received an award for her performance in the film Grace de Monaco at the Taormina Festival.

The actress, who at the time described her work in the film as “a dream come true”, received her award with a huge smile before her husband who watched with pride in his eyes.

After 14 years of living in Los Angeles, Paz Vega has settled with her husband and three children in Madrid. The actress has nothing but words of satisfaction about the decision made after living in the United States for years. “The idea is to stay, the children are delighted, my husband and I are happy to reunite with our friends, our customs and we are very happy. I will be coming and going because I have work there, but happy to return home, HOLA reported.
