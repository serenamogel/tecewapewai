Ana Ortiz is an American actress and singer by profession. She is best known for being a talented, well-trained star as she is blessed with adorable acting skills and a mellifluous voice. She has been in this field since a young age. She completed her studies at the University of the Arts. Ana Ortiz is also known for her admiring work and dedicated performances.

Career

In the early 2000s, Ana Ortiz started off her acting career with theatre. She made an appearance in the short-lived NNBC sitcoms Kristin (2001) for which she earned much praise and recognition in her life.

Among other notable works, Ana Ortiz became famous for appearing in ‘A.U.S.A’ (2003), and other well-appreciated films and TV series are prominent on the list.

Additionally, she became famous for appearing in a role on ‘Over There and Boston Legal’. Undoubtedly, her performance was well-praised by many.

Ortiz gained wider attention for giving an admiring performance in roles as a Hilda Suarez in the ABC comedy-drama series Ugly Betty from 2006 to 2010.

Also, she made appearances in movies, like; Labor Pains (2009) and Big Mommas: Like Father, Like Son (2011), and many more.

In addition to these projects, she also gained fame for starring in Little Girl Lost: The Delimar Vera Story (2008).

Achievements

The actress also won the Imagen Award for Best Actress-Televison for the Lifetime Television comedy-drama series named ‘Devious Maids'(2013-2016).
